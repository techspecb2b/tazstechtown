About Tazs Tech Town

Tazs Tech Town was built to make technology repair simple, fast, and convenient. We believe you shouldn’t have to unplug your life just to fix your devices.

That’s why we operate as a 100% mobile repair service. We come directly to you—whether it’s your home, workplace, or anywhere else.

For repairs that require extra care, we offer secure pickup, professional repair, and prompt drop-off.
